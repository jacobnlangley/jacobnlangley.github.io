jacØb published on 2022-02-25
“There is no first moment, no single word or place from which this or any other story begins… …It must be arbitrary, then the place at which we choose to embark. This place for instance.” This passage begins Weaveworld, by Clive Barker. I read this book many years ago as a young twenty-something. I have always loved this beginning. This embarkation. For some reason I have always remembered these words.
